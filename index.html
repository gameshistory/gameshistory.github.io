Home

Welcome to my website. It is basically a listing of retro video games that I enjoy or appreciate.

I started by documenting every noteworthy video game released in the 1970s. I wanted to get an idea of where many video game concepts originate.

Following this, I deep-dived into the Atari 8-bit's library and made a video on it. This helped paint a picture of what gaming was like before Super Mario and Zelda.

From there, I have been going through all console games from the NES and onwards. I have also been going through computer games of this era too - much less thouroghly.

The last section of my website - Miscellaneous - is just where I store everything else. It has my top five games, favorite critic, some articles, and other ramblings. Hope you enjoy.
